https://dbrown.id.au/arcanum-windows-10.html

	1. 下載 https://assets.dbrown.id.au/arcanum-windows-10/UAP091225.exe
		安裝位置為(第二層) F:\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Arcanum\Arcanum\


	2. 下載 https://assets.dbrown.id.au/arcanum-windows-10/HighRes1.5.zip
		安裝位置為(第二層) 複製資料夾至 F:\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Arcanum\Arcanum\HighRes\
		修改 config.ini 但 DDrawWrapper 必須為 0 
		執行 _install.bat


	3. 安裝 https://assets.dbrown.id.au/arcanum-windows-10/DxWrapper.zip
		解壓縮到 (第二層) F:\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Arcanum\Arcanum\
		蓋掉原來ddraw.dll
		將settings.ini 裡面 DDrawCompat 改為 1
		將setiings.ini 改名為 ddraw.ini
