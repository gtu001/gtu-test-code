How to enable VR in Euro Truck Simulator 2

	1.按右鍵選properties
	2.選BETAS頁簽
	3.選第一個下拉 oculus - Oculus/Vive - 1.35 - (SDK 1.4.0)
	4.選GENERAL頁簽
	5.勾掉Use Desktop Game Theatre while SteamVR is active
	6.點按鈕SET LAUNCH OPTIONS
	7.輸入-openvr

	Ps : 按F11切換VR模式
